Asked by Sarah Johnson 2 hours ago 156 views

I'm a first-year student and I'm trying to understand the bursary application process. I've heard that there are different types of bursaries available, but I'm not sure which ones I might qualify for or how to apply.

Specifically, I'd like to know:

  • What are the eligibility requirements?
  • When is the application deadline?
  • What documents do I need to submit?
  • Where can I find the application form?

The bursary application process is actually quite straightforward once you know where to look. Here's a comprehensive guide:

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Must be a registered student
  • Household income below R350,000 per annum
  • Minimum academic average of 60%

Application Deadline: March 31st for the upcoming academic year

Required Documents:

  • Certified ID copy
  • Proof of income (payslips or affidavit)
  • Academic transcript
  • Proof of residence

Application Form: You can download it from the student portal under Financial Aid section, or visit the Bursary Office in Building C, Room 204.

Good luck with your application!

I just went through this process last semester! In addition to what was mentioned, I'd recommend:

  • Start early - don't wait until the last minute
  • Make sure all documents are certified within the last 3 months
  • Keep copies of everything you submit
  • Follow up after 2 weeks if you haven't heard back

The staff at the Bursary Office are really helpful, so don't hesitate to ask questions!
